Acoustika



Accoustika live in Qatar at Regency halls, Doha. This was indeed the first live concert organized by Bluebell International in Qatar. The show featured Jyotsna Radhakrishnan, one of the well known playback singer in the Malayalam film industry along with musicians Ralfin Stephen, Santosh Chandran, Ben Sam Jones, Anish and Nikhil. Accoustika was one of the best live concerts where the performance engaged a huge audience of 1200+. Besides the musical extravaganza there were games, fun shows and gifts organized to entertain the audience.


Mall of Asia's Grand Opening



Bluebell international organized the grand opening of Mall of Asia at Industrial area, Doha. A grand Onam celebration was also organized along with the inaugural function. There were 500+ people present to witness the opening and there were other shows arranged to keep people engaged. Cultural programmes for kids Quiz sessions and magic shows were highlights of the programme.


Grand Mall's Inaugral Ceremony

Legends - Live In Qatar


Bluebell International organized a live music concert with the legends of the Indian music industry at Al Arabi Sports Club. Legends live in Qatar featured the renowned playback singers S. P. Balasubrahmanyam & K.S Chithra live to the music lovers of Qatar. It was indeed one of the best live concerts with a huge audience of 5000+. Both the maestros entertained the crowd with their melodies thus creating a memorable experience to their fans. In addition to the musical night there was a raffle draw and gifts were distributed.


Shankar Ehsaan Loy


Bluebell International organized a live music concert with the Indian musical trio Shankar-Ehsaan-Loy for the first time in Qatar at Asian Town Amphitheater. It was an extremely entertaining show featuring the well known musician Shankar Mahadevan and the composers Ehsaan Noorani and Loy Mendonsa. This was indeed one of the biggest shows that we have organized and Qatar has ever witnessed with over 15,000 spectators. There was also a raffle draw conducted with the event.


Thaikkudam Bridge Live


Thaikkudam bridge live in Qatar at Doha Marriott hotel's Open air ground. This live concert had a huge audience of over 3000 people and there was also a raffle draw.
